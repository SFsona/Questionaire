AUDIO VIDEO
video sources

When watching video sources and content (such as Sky, AppleTV or streaming services such as Netflix & Disney+), there are two main ways of accessing these sources:

Source devices are local to the room, and connected directly to the display, or
Source devices are centrally located, and users select their own device for viewing which follows them from room to room.
Local sources require less hardware equipment and are the more cost effective option.
Centrally located sources offer significantly more flexibility and the ability to synchonise viewing across multiple rooms, but are a significantly more costly option.
